WHO AM I?

SOFTWARE DEVELOPER

Graduating from UCSD with a bachelors in Human Biology, it seemed it wasn't long until I went to medical school as my parents wished. Until the moment I realized.. why am I doing what they want and not what I want? Who are they to dictate what I will be doing for the rest of my life long after they have passed? So I made the decision to quit the pre-med life and haven't looked back since.

I received my formal training in software development at App Academy. It was a rigorous and immersive full-stack program teaching Ruby on Rails, Javascript, Backbone.js, jQuery, SQL, HTML, and CSS. The rapid pace of learning and hands-on exercises enabled me to take my passion to the level where I can do what I love as a career.

I am currently seeking full-time employment as a web developer in the San Francisco bay area. I'm an easy going guy with a great sense of humor looking to surround myself with companies also sharing these similar characteristics.

PORTFOLIO

AGRO

AGRO (Authentic Game Reviews Online) enables avid gamers to collaborate on finding the most fulfilling video game and act as a primary resource for future visitors of the site. Video games are a growing form of entertainment and with new games developed constantly, gamers are always searching for a game matching their taste. Current game review websites seem to publish reviews by critics not in the same age category as players and don't necessarily have to same opinion as the players themselves. AGRO serves as an open platform for individuals to rate games truthfully and also provide honest information simultaneously. The site is built upon the community in an effort to serve the community.
